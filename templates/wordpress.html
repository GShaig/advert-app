What is WordPress?

WordPress is the most popular and the most used content management system in the world. The statistics shows that around forty three percent of all websites on the internet were built on WordPress CMS as of 2023. And the demand for WordPress is increasing day by day. Today we are going to explain why WordPress is so popular and why most of the bloggers choose it to develop their own websites. Advantages of WordPress CMS over other web development methods.

The biggest advantage of WordPress CMS is that you do not need extensive coding skills to use it and develop your own website. Even people without web development knowledge and experience can easily build a functional website with the help of WordPress CMS. You just need to create your account and start using the content management system for building your blog or any other kind of website.

Another advantage of WordPress CMS is being open source and free for everyone on the web. You do not have to purchase a license or pay monthly subscription fee to be able to use WordPress CMS. Most of the plugins and themes on wordpress.org also have free versions to use without buying them. WordPress CMS even provides a domain name for your website, but it is kind of a subdomain of wordpress.org. If you bought a domain name form a different domain registrar before, you can easily connect that domain with your WordPress website.

Moreover, WordPress CMS have easy to use website builder and user interface for a better and productive website development experience. On the main dashboard, you can access every section such as Themes, Plugins, Media, Posts, and Pages. With a plugin named Elementor, you can even edit your web pages with visual editor by seeing the reflection of the changes you have made real time. There are also security and anti fraud plugins like limit login attempts and antispam bee.





What is a Plugin on WordPress?

A plugin on WordPress is a collection of codes prepared for adding extra features to your website. You can browse a list of plugins or make a search for a specific plugin on wordpress.org with ease. Just navigate to Plugins and click on Add New to add the plugin you are looking for to your website. You first need to install and activate the plugin before using it. Also do not forget to go to the settings for that plugin and setup the plugin correctly after activating it. To do that, you will need to navigate to Installed Plugins section which will show the list of installed plugins for your website, find the plugin on the list, and click on Settings button next to the Activate button for the plugin.



What is a Theme for a WordPress website?

A theme is like a skeleton for your website. It is a general layout and design for a website. You can change and adjust the theme according to your wishes after you successfully installed and activated it. To see the list of already installed themes for your website, navigate to the Appearance tab and from the drop down menu, click on the Themes button. To preview or activate any of the themes, click on the Live Preview or Activate button under the theme name. To install a new theme, click on the Add New button on the top. Browse the list of available themes, choose your preferred one and click on the Install button under the theme name in order to install and apply the theme to your website. If you wish to edit the existing theme of your website, navigate to Appearance tab, then click on the Customize button. This button will open a new window for you where you can see your website as any user on the Internet will see it and make changes to your theme, as well as see the instant reflections of your changes on the screen.





What is WooCommerce?

WooCommerce is a plugin mostly used by e-commerce websites. It allows you to list your products, accept payments online and manage your orders. It is the number one plugin solution for online selling. When you install and activate WooCommerce plugin, it will add five sections to your WordPress dashboard: WooCommerce, Products, Payments, Analytics, and Marketing sections. You can find the general settings on the WooCommerce section including payment settings and settings for the products and shipping. On the Products section, you cand find the list of added products and add new products for your online shop. You can see your available balance and the next deposit date on the Payments section.



The most important sections for building your own website.

In this part, we will talk about the most common sections on WordPress CMS that you will most likely encounter daily in your website building journey with WordPress. The first section you will mostly visit very frequently is Themes section. This section is used to install new themes or change the current theme of your website. Another section can be Plugins where you can add new plugins or activate / deactivate existing plugins for your website. Additionally, you will visit the Pages section where you can see the list of published web pages, as well as add relevant content to the pages. The next section might be Customize section to change the appearance of your website. Occasionally, you will visit Media section to add new images and videos to be published on your website. And if you built and manage a blog website, you will go to the Posts section to write your daily or weekly blogs for the website. Please note that all the above instructions are initial tips and guides to help you to start your WordPress website.
