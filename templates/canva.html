Canva




What is Canva?

Canva is a software as a service (SAAS) platform to be used for photo editing, background removal, banner design, and graphic design works. It is the number one website preferred by many site owners and bloggers for preparing attractive and amazing posts. Canva is an exceptional tool with a lot of free clip arts, backgrounds, and frames. Using Canva is very easy and does not require any graphic design skill. You just need to sign up and create an account on Canva.com. You will see everything on your easy to use dashboard. You can create a project, work on it, or save your works to work on the project later. The best part is that you can even work on your project as a team and add your team members to the projects you wish to work together. We will talk about different features of Canva in this article.



Is Canva free of charge?

Creating an account and start projects on Canva.com is free. You can also work on your projects, add free versions of clip arts and images, and even download your designs and projects in a limited number of file formats and with limited resolution options. The free version of the platform is also very functional. You can create and download documents, presentations, Instagram posts and stories, Facebook posts, logos, posters, and videos. You can also create a whiteboard, customize it as per your requirements and print it as a PNG file. But the paid version includes even more advanced features such as download your projects as a scalable vector graphic (SVG) file, or use premium elements in your projects like premium templates, shapes, clip arts, graphics, stickers, photos and videos. Paid membership for premium features is not expensive, it is only 15 dollars per month per person. Most of the social media posts, blog posts and memes you see on social media platforms were created with the free version of Canva.





What can you do on Canva.com?

- Create the media to use on your blog articles: The best tool on the Internet to create your photos, graphs and other visualisations for your blog posts is Canva. Using its graphics, shapes and feature rich editor gives you the freedom to create the best visuals for your articles with unique designs.

- Create Instagram posts: Sharing effective and attractive posts is the gateway to the success for your Instagram profile. In order to increase the number of your followers day by day, you should post at least in every other day. This means that you have to share a lot of content. And for Instagram, the content means an image or a graph. This is a lot of content and Canva is a single tool where you can create all these contents with minimal time.

- Create a logo for your brand: Creating a logo for your brand name can be a hassle for many business owners. If you are a small business owner and do not have a big budget and enough time to create your logo from scratch, Canva can be a solution for you. It has ready designs for effective logo design and premium shapes, letter combinations to use in your logo. You can also use functional and free logo maker tool as part of Canva.

- Create posters for your business: Canva is an easy way to create colourful and high resolution posters to be printed out to use as hard copies. Photo resolution up to 8000 pixels provide very good photo quality even in huge poster sizes. There is even a free poster maker tool which you can use to create attention catching posters for your business.

- Create presentations and documents: It is very easy to create productive presentations and high quality documents with Canva. You have the option to save your documents as PDF files. The platform has powerful tools to create content rich slides and put astonishing transitions between them.

- Create memes for personal use: Canva is a helpful tool also to create your own memes. You can create and share cartoons about popular topics in today's world. Sharing funny and entertaining posts is another method to increase your social media presence.

- Create flyers with your logo and brand name to be distributed in your local business area. You can create your flyers with the help of free flyer maker tool.

- Canva is one of the best services on the market to create attractive banners to be placed on your website as banner ads. It offers free banner maker tool to make it easier for you to create your banners by providing the necessary assistance.

- You can create an amazing CV for you with the help of free Canva resume maker.
