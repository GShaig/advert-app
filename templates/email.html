Business Email FAQs


What is a business email and how can I obtain it?

Business email is an email address in your own custom domain name. Generic format for business email is info@yourdomain.com.

There are many business email providers in the market. You should find a good service provider in order to get the most out of your business email package.


Is there a free option to get a business email?

Yes. There are some web hosting providers and online service providers (like Zoho mail) that offer a limited number of business email accounts for free.

Usually free packages are very limited for both mailbox size and premium features (like spam filtering, backup, email encryption etc.).


What makes a business email so important?

There are many factors which makes a business email essential for your business. It looks professional and your company gets the trust of your potential clients in the first step.

Business email plan gives you flexibility and control over your communication with your customers. You can create an email account under the name of each employee and introduce them to the clients at the first glance.


Can an individual have a business email?

Of course, yes. It is also beneficial for individuals to have a business email in order to leave a good impression in your clients. If you are operating individually or you are a self employed professional, you also need a good online presence in order to turn your potential customers into your loyal clients.

In these days many people run their individual businesses online or work as a freelancer offering their services. In any case, having a business email is a big advantage against your competitors and gives you freedom and self-confidence in relations with your clients.
