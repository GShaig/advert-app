Top seven SEO tips for your domain name.

Keywords: Use important keywords in your domain name. For example, tool, digital, and free are some of them. Use a keyword research tool like Google Keyword Planner to get a better idea about the monthly search volumes for your keywords.

Domain Authority: Domain authority is the reputation of your domain name. Measured with 100 points scale from 0 to 100. Your domain authority depends on how many backlinks you have on the web. A backlink is a link on other website directing their traffic to your website. Having a backlink from a reputed website increases your domain authority significantly.

Having an SSL certificate: If you have an SSL certificate covering your domain and subdomains that means you have greater chances to have a high SEO ranking. HTTPS enabled domains are catching the attention of search engines.

Choosing .com domain extension: It is undeniable that .com domain extension has a big impact on your SEO ranking. If you wish to buy the exact domain name which is in your mind and the domain is not available with .com extension, try to get it with .net or .org extensions.

Keeping short: You should try your best to make your domain name as short as possible. Sort domain names are more memorable and therefore attracts more traffic compare to the long ones.

Avoiding non-letter characters: Avoid numbers and hyphens when choosing your domain name. This will definitely help you for your SEO.

Getting help: Get help from domain generators or other artificial intelligence tools on the internet when brainstorming about your domain name. Just type your most important keywords and let the tool suggest you different options with various domain extensions. Grab the best and most suited option for you!

The most interesting facts about domain names.

- I have seen that my domain name is registered under the name of my domain registrar. Can it be a problem for me? Short answer is No. Domain registrars usually register a domain name under their name on behalf of their clients. I does not posses any problem for you and domain registrars sometimes extend a domain registration for themselves even the registration period for the client is already expired.

- Is www.mywebsite.com and mywebsite.com the same web page in terms of URL routing? No, www.yourwebsite.com is considered a subdomain of your website. Therefore, you have to state a separate routing condition for www. prefix. Web developers usually direct the www.mywebsite.com subdomain of their website to the root domain (mywebsite.com) of the website.

- A price of a domain name increases depending on how many premium keywords the domain includes. For example, adverttools.com contains two popular keywords: advert and tool. But do not tend to buy long domain names with multiple keywords because a domain name value decreases with the domain length.

- If the domain name you are looking for is already registered, try plural keywords instead of singular ones. For instance, search for prettyflowers.com in place of prettyflower.com or amazingreviews.com as an alternative to amazingreview.com. You can also use the same keywords without vowels, like projectmngmnt.com instead of projectmanagement.com.



FAQs


How do I check a domain name availability?

If you want to know whether a domain name available for purchase or not, please use our Domain Search Tool.

Type in the domain name in the search box and click on "Search".


What if the domain name I look for is already registered?

If the domain name you wish to buy is already registered, you can push WHOIS button next to the domain name and learn the current owner of the domain and his / her contact details.

If getting that specific domain name is very important for you, you can contact with the domain owner and ask if he / she wants to sell the domain name or not.


I wish to buy a domain name for my business or company, but the exact domain name is already registered. What to do?

Try to add extra words like the, company or shop into the domain name and search again. You can also look for different TLDs for the same domain name.

For example, if the domain name you checked before was with .COM extension, make a search with .NET, .ORG or .CO domain extensions.


Where I can get domain name ideas for my website or business?

You can check your competitor domains to have an idea how to determine your own domain name. Take into account important keywords in your industry.

Additionally, you can use free domain name generator tools from the internet. One of the best domain generators on the internet is Namecheap Domain Generator. You can reach and use it by clicking on Domain Name Generator.
