How to use URL Shortener Tool?

Using our URL shortener tool is as easy as copy paste action. Just copy the URL you want to make short and paste it inside the search box provided above. Then click on Shorten button and that's it!

Wait for 1 second and your shortened link will be ready for you! You can copy and share it with your friends or put it on your social media posts such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, YouTube or Reddit posts!

Top five tips for being on the safe side when using a URL shortener.

Avoid domains look like scams. Do not jump in to a URL shortener tool with a scammy domain name like shooortlinks.com or shorturlll.net. This kind of websites will probably create a link to a scam or malicious resources and will hurt you or your friends and audience.

Test your short link immediately after creating it. Be the first user of your newly generated short link. But be careful! Enter your short link only in incognito window and if the link directs you to a different website than your original one, do not click on any button on the website. Immediately close the window and never ever visit that website again.

Use a link scam or phishing checker tool. There are tons of URL checker tools on the internet. After creating your short link on a URL shortener, just use one of the genuine link checker services to be sure of that the link is safe to use for you and your audience.

Use HTTPS enabled and secured websites. Use URL shortener tools only on the HTTPS enabled and SSL certificate installed websites. Because in this way, every time a user clicks on your short link, the transaction between the user and the final destination website will be encrypted and secured. For your information, adverttools.com is HTTPS enabled and protected with a wildcard SSL certificate.

Be careful about the website you use. Create your short links only with the URL shortener on a genuine and trusted website. And do not use temporary websites. Otherwise, the website might become unavailable or offline after some time. If this is the case, your short links will be broken or will throw 404 - Not Found error every time they are used.
