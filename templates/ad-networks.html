What is an AD Network?




What is an ad network and how does it work?

Ad network is a platform between advertisers and publishers. Publishers are people who own a website and wish to monetize their websites by selling ad spaces from the website. Advertisers are business owner who are looking for ad platforms to promote their businesses. The working principle of an ad network is very simple. It gets paid by business owners and pays the amount to its publishers by keeping a portion of the payment for itself. A usual ad network takes from 10% up to 15% of the payment on average. But this amount can be higher in different ad networks.



What are the most popular ad networks in 2023?

The most popular ad networks in 2023 can be listed as below: 1. Google AdSense 2. Ezoic 3. Media.net 4. Propeller Ads 5. Criteo Ads

Google AdSense is the largest and most popular ad network in the world. There are many website owners, publishers and bloggers who monetize their website or blog with Google AdSense. AdSense has the widest base of advertisers and also earnings from AdSense is the well above of the market average. AdSense keeps 32 percent of the revenue generated by the ads it provides on your website. Even though you get only 68 percent of the total revenue, it is still the highest paid option when you think about monetizing your site with the help of ad networks.



How to monetize your website with ad networks?

The starting point for this business structure is obviously building a website which is compliant with all the requirements that common ad networks enforce. First of all, you need to determine which kind of website you wish to build for this purpose. There are usually three kind of websites available for ad network monetization: a blog website, a free tools website or a forum website. After deciding what type of website you want to build, the next step will be choosing your niche. Keep in mind that you should choose the niche which you possess an extensive knowledge about it or at least you are familiar with the basics of it. We will not dive in the topics about web development here because you can get deep knowledge about web development by reading other articles published on adverttools.com.





What are the important requirements that implemented by todayâ€™s ad networks?

In order to be accepted by the popular ad networks these days, there mainly four types of requirements: content requirements, traffic requirements, language requirements and navigation requirements. Commonly accepted content requirements include having enough content on your website, the quality and uniqueness of your content, avoiding forbidden content like pornography, discrimination, or violence, and having a set niche for your content. The traffic requirement is usually bound with ten thousand page views per month. Each of ad networks has its own list of supported languages. Your website must be in the language which is in the supported languages list of your preferred ad network. Navigation requirement means that you must have a clear and functional navigation menu on your website.



What are the common pages you should have on your website?

Some ad networks require that you must have About Us, Terms and Conditions, Privacy Policy, and Contact Us pages. In About Us page, you should have enough information about you and your website for users. You should include at least one active email address or a contact form on your Contact Us page. Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions pages also should include the relevant information about your website. It is not hard to fill these pages. There are plenty of Privacy Policy and Terms of Service generators on the web.



How to apply to your preferred ad network?

Make your research and choose the most suitable ad network for your website. Head to the website of the ad network and create an account for you. Confirm your email and phone number with the verification methods provided on the website. Then you will have to verify your ownership for the website you claim to put ads on. Usually this is done either by adding a CNAME record to the DNS settings of your domain name or by putting an HTML code in between the head tags on the base HTML template of your website. To verify your ownership is fairly easy compared to getting approval for ad serving from the ad network. After verifying your ownership, click on the "Request Review" or "Submit" button to start the evaluation process by your ad network. The platform will analyze your website and get back to you with the decision they have made for your website. This process usually takes from a couple of days up to a month. If your website was rejected by the ad network, there is usually a note attached to the outcome explaining the reason why your website was rejected. Do not reapply immediately after the rejection. Analyze and rectify the issue provided on the note before you apply again to get an approval from the ad network as soon as possible.
