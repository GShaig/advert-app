Which monetization method is the best to buy with a website?

When you browse a list of websites on flippa.com, you can see that the monetization method for different websites varies greatly. Some of the websites are monetized with the combination of available monetization methods. For example, one website can be monetized with both affiliate sales and ad revenue at the same time. But which monetization method is the best to purchase a website with? The answer to this question depends on your situation and each of the monetization methods has its own risks to deal with. For example, having a thousand dollars revenue from affiliate sales per month for a specific website does not guarantee that you will receive the same sales amount each month from that website. The current owner of the website may follow a special sales strategy to reach that amount every single month. So, if you do not have the knowledge to implement a similar sales strategy, you may be disappointed with the result. Similarly, if a website gets two thousand dollars ad revenue per month, this does not mean that you will receive the same amount. For such situation, the website owner will probably not pass you his / her AdSense and other ad platforms accounts which means you will have to apply to the same ad platforms again by creating your own account on each ad platform. As a result, it is not guaranteed that your newly created account and your new website will be approved by the relevant ad platform authorities. But there is one thing that is guaranteed that you will receive it: the organic traffic that the website receives each month. But be careful about that. Check and confirm that the organic traffic information about the website is verified by flippa.com. Otherwise, you will risk your money and your efforts for a false information provided by the website owner. Please note that transferring an ecommerce or other kind of monetized website is a long process and requires a lot of patient from both buyer and seller. For this reason, you may want to develop your own website rather than buying one. It is also worth checking the production platform of the website and the programming language the website was built with. Because if you do not have knowledge of the programming language or the production environment of the website, that means you will not be able to manage and update that website yourself.
