What is Business Email?




What is a Business Email and why it is important?

Business email is a custom email address without generic hotmail.com, gmail.com, yahoo.com, or mail.ru extensions. Business emails are usually created under your business name or domain name like name@example.com.

It is very important to have a business email in order to look professional and serious on the web. One option to have a custom email address is to buy a custom domain name and create email accounts under that domain name. But in this option, the number of accounts you can create or the size of a single mailbox is limited.

Another way to have a business name is to buy it from a service provider like Namecheap, Microsoft Outlook 365, Google Workspace etc. Our Business Email Advisor tool gets the necessary information from you and advises you the best and the most cost-effective business email package for you.

If you wish to buy the package, the tool directs you to Namecheap website where you can complete your purchase.

Business email is not used just for reputation. Using custom email addresses provides better authentication and prevents your emails end up in the spam or junk folder. Additionally, some service providers for your business may require you to have a business email before you can create your account or register for the service.

In terms of business and communications, custom email addresses will help you a lot. It is good for both internal and external communications in your organization. You can create tailored email addresses for your staff members and different departments. This will increase productivity and lead to better communication within the company.

When you buy a business email package, please consider a service provider with important features such as spam filtering and mailbox backups. Security is also important in order to protect any confidential information in your emails and prevent you from cyber attacks as your username and password can be stolen.





What are the most common business email addresses for companies?

info@yourcompany.com – If you have only one business email, it should definitely be this one. Info covers most of the processes and procedures in your company, being it a simple information request or an important business negotiation.

admin@yourcompany.com – This email address is usually belongs to the website administrator. But some companies give this email address to their administrators in different departments.

office@yourcompany.com – This email address represents your office and usually held by the company secretary. This email address can handle emails for the communications with the head office.

accounts@yourcompany.com – As you can easily guess, this email address belongs to accounting or finance department or simply to the person work as a company accountant.

inquiries@yourcompany.com – This email address usually handles general inquiries and the person managing this mailbox can direct the inquiries to the relevant departments.

sales@yourcompany.com – This email address is usually managed by a sales team with each team member has access and authority to send and receive emails for sales activities.

operations@yourcompany.com – This special email address is meant to be used in big companies where operations, management, and other departments are totally separated from each other.



Can I get a business email free?

If you already have a registered domain name, you may obtain a business email free of charge. Especially if you bought a hosting plan with your domain, you will probably be able to create one or more (depending on a hosting provider) business email accounts under your domain name.

Some websites also provide free custom email accounts if you own a domain name. But provided mailbox is usually with limited capacity and mostly lacks of premium features that most service providers supply like spam filtering, high storage, daily backups and email encryption.



How to create email accounts on your hosting plan.

Please note that in order to follow below steps, you need to have a hosting plan and a custom domain name attached to it.

1. Enter your dashboard by providing your username and password credentials.

2. Navigate to the domains section.

3. Click on the Create a new email account button.

4. Choose a username for your email account. For instance, if you wish to create an email account like info@yourdomain.com , put info in the box provided for your email account. You can also create an email account including your name and surname just as name.surname@yourdomain.com.

5. Choose a password for your email account and hit the Submit button once you filled the form for email account registration.

6. After you successfully created your email account, wait for a couple of minutes for the email address setup to be finished.

7. After approximately five minutes you will be able to use your email account. Just go to the webmail address of your hosting provider like mail.hostingprovider.com and enter your username and password you chose when you created your email account. Then you will be directed to the inbox of your new email address!
